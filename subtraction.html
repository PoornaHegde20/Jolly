SUBTRACTION!!

Let's learn subtraction of numbers!

Subtraction can be visualized as 'removing' a quantity from another quantity. A number's value is
taken away from the other number, and the answer is what is left of that number. The sign for
subtraction is - (minus). This is one of the four arithmetic operations.
