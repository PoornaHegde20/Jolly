COMPARISON

Let's learn comparison of numbers!

GREATER THAN

A number is always greater than every number preceding it! Let's consider the number 5. The numbers preceding it are 4, 3, 2 and 1. That means, 5 is greater than all these numbers! The symbol for greater than is ">".
Eg: 8>5, 4>1.

LESSER THAN

A number is always lesser than the numbers succeeding it! Let's consider the number 5. The numbers succeeding it are 6, 7, 8, etc. That means, 5 is lesser than all these numbers! The symbol for lesser than is "<".
Eg:5<10, 1<3.

You'll be higher on the seesaw if you're greater than the other number!!
